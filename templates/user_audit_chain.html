🔐 How This Audit Chain Works

🔗

Cryptographic Linking

Each block contains the hash of the previous block, creating an unbreakable chain. If any block is modified, the entire chain becomes invalid.

⛏️

Proof of Work

Each block requires computational work (mining) to create, making tampering computationally expensive and time-consuming.

🔒

Tamper Detection

Any modification to a block's data will change its hash, breaking the chain and immediately alerting administrators to tampering.

📊

Complete Audit

Every critical action is permanently recorded with timestamp, details, and cryptographic proof of authenticity.
